FOODIE LIFE is an application which helps you to maintain the record of food items stored in your "REFERIGERATOR" and helps to track expiry of food items to avoid wastage

FOOD INVENTORY

'FOODIE LIFE' Helps people to save the record of the food items bought by them with the quantity and expiry dates of the respective item. Also help to know the storage available in their fridge

SMART RECIPES

'FOODIE LIFE' Matches the stocked products available in the fridge and it helps to scale the recipe and add the missing ingredients to your shopping list. Discover and import easy, simple recipes related to ingredeints whose expiry has almost there.

EXPIRY TRACKING

FOODIE LIFE's unique expiry tracking system help's to avoid food waste driving habits, such as expiry labels confusion, premature binning, overbuying, etc..
